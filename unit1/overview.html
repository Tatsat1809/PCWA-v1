Unit 1: Unit Circle

Overview

Definition of Unit Circle

The unit circle with a radius of 1, centred at the origin (0,0) of the cordinate plane.

x2 + y2 = 1

Every point P(x, y) that lies on the circle satisfies this equation. The unit circle forms the foundation for defining the trigonometric functions sine, cosine and tangent

Angles & Radians

Angles can be measured in degrees or radians

  • Degrees divide a circle into 360 equal parts
  • Radians measure angles in terms of the arc length of the circle
